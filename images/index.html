Images

Images should be uploaded in as high resolution as possible (the CMS will resize and compress appropriately).

The best images show people’s faces and show people that are empowered. Be careful that photos don’t suggest a dated understanding of aid or reinforce gender and racial stereotypes.

Alt text

Alt text should describe the image in a brief sentence or two so that people who don’t see the image should come away with the same information as if they had.

Three smiling people standing under a tree.

Caption

Text that explains an image or gives additional context. Captions can give information that isn’t necessarily obvious from the image itself.

Community activists Bilal, Mwanahamis and Adinani in Tanzania.

Credit

Text that says who took a photo and whether it’s copyright. Where a photo is licensed under a creative commons licence, the credit should also include a link to the licence.
