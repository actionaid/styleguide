Brand personality traits

ActionAid International is all of these things (not necessarily all at the same time).

1. A modern thinker.

We are thoughtful and insightful but not elitist. Opinionated but still approachable. Youthful and in touch.

2. A brave writer.

Unafraid of authority and a pricker of pomposity. Confident using the power of evidence to cut to the heart.

3. A no-nonsense straight-talker.

A rocker of boats, a holder to account, a challenger of the status quo. Gritty and raw.

4. An insightful confidante.

Sensitive and empathetic, but not a mincer of words. Respects dignity. Empowers by offering a platform to under-represented voices.
